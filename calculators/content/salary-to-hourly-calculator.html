What is Salary to Hourly Conversion?

Salary to hourly conversion is the breakdown of an annual, monthly, or weekly fixed pay into an equivalent hourly wage. This is essential for understanding your "true" rate of pay, especially if you find yourself working more than the standard 40-hour week. It is a critical metric for work-life balance and fair compensation.

Formula

Hourly Rate = Annual Salary / (52 × Hours per Week)

Example: ₹6,00,000 / 2,080 hours = ~₹288/hour

Example

Scenario: You earn ₹8,00,000 per year and work a standard 40-hour week.

  • Annual Salary = ₹8,00,000
  • Total Annual Hours = 40 × 52 = 2,080
  • Hourly Rate = ₹8,00,000 / 2,080 = ~₹384.62
