Every workout burns energy — but how much? Our calorie burn calculator estimates the exact calories you burn during any physical activity based on your weight, exercise type, and duration, helping you reach your fitness goals with precise data.

What is a Calorie Burn Calculator?

A calorie burn calculator uses the MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) system to estimate energy expenditure during exercise. Each activity has a MET value representing how much energy it uses relative to sitting still. Combined with your body weight and exercise duration, it gives a scientifically-based calorie burn estimate.

Formula

Calories Burned = MET × Weight (kg) × Duration (hours)

Running MET ≈ 8-10 | Cycling ≈ 6-8 | Swimming ≈ 5-7 | Walking ≈ 3-4 | Yoga ≈ 2.5-3

Example Calculation

70 kg person runs for 45 minutes (MET = 8).

  • Duration in hours = 45/60 = 0.75 hours
  • Calories = 8 × 70 × 0.75 = 420 calories

FAQs

Is this calculator accurate for everyone?

MET-based estimates have ±10-15% individual variation depending on fitness level, metabolism, and exercise intensity. It's an excellent guideline, but wearable fitness trackers provide more personalised data.

Which exercise burns the most calories?

Running and HIIT typically burn the most calories per hour. Swimming is the most joint-friendly high-calorie burn activity. Cycling is efficient for endurance athletes.
