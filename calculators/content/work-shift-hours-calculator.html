What is a Work Shift Hours Calculator?

A work shift calculator determines how many hours and minutes are in a given work shift by calculating the difference between shift start and end times, then subtracting any unpaid break time. It handles overnight shifts that cross midnight and can compute weekly totals for payroll verification.

Formula

Gross Shift Duration = End Time − Start Time

Net Work Hours = Gross Duration − Break Duration

For overnight: If End Time < Start Time, add 24 hours to End Time before subtracting.

Example Calculation

  • Start: 8:00 AM. End: 8:00 PM. Break: 60 minutes.
  • Gross = 12 hours. Net = 12 − 1 = 11 hours
  • Overnight: Start 10:00 PM, End 6:30 AM → Gross = 8h 30m. Net (30min break) = 8 hours

FAQs

What are the standard shift types in India?

General shift: 9 AM-6 PM (9 hrs). Day shift: 6 AM-2 PM. Evening shift: 2 PM-10 PM. Night shift: 10 PM-6 AM. Most factories operate three 8-hour shifts to run 24/7.

Are breaks counted as working hours?

Unpaid breaks (like lunch breaks) are not counted as work hours for payment purposes. Paid breaks (like tea breaks in some union agreements) may be included depending on your employment contract.
