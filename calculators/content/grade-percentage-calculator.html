What is Grade Percentage?

A grade percentage is a numerical representation of an academic score relative to the total possible points. It standardizes scores across different assignments, making it easy to see where you stand. While many schools use letter grades (A, B, C), the percentage is the foundation used to determine those letters.

Formula

Grade (%) = (Points Earned / Total Points) × 100

Example: (45 / 50) × 100 = 90%

Example

Scenario: You got 18 out of 25 on a biology quiz.

  • Points Earned = 18
  • Possible Points = 25
  • Calculation = (18 / 25) × 100 = 0.72 × 100
  • Grade Percentage = 72%

How to Use

  1. Points Earned: The score you received on the assignment.
  2. Possible Points: The maximum marks available for the assignment.
  3. Click Calculate: Instantly see your score as a clean percentage.
