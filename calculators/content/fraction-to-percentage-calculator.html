What is Fraction to Percentage Conversion?

A fraction represents a part of a whole as a ratio of two numbers (numerator/denominator). Converting it to a percentage expresses that same proportion on a scale of 100, making it easier to compare, communicate, and apply in real-world calculations like exam scores, success rates, and financial ratios.

Formula

Percentage = (Numerator / Denominator) × 100

Example Calculation

  • 3/4 = (3 ÷ 4) × 100 = 75%
  • 7/8 = (7 ÷ 8) × 100 = 87.5%
  • 2/3 = (2 ÷ 3) × 100 = 66.67%
  • 5/6 = (5 ÷ 6) × 100 = 83.33%

FAQs

How do I convert a percentage back to a fraction?

Divide the percentage by 100 and simplify. Example: 75% = 75/100 = 3/4.

What is 3/5 as a percentage?

(3 ÷ 5) × 100 = 60%.
