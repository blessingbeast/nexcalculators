What is a Percentage of Number Calculator?

This calculator finds what a given percentage of any number equals. You input the percentage (X%) and the number (N), and it returns the exact value. It's used for discount calculations, tax computation, commission, exam mark analysis, and almost every financial calculation in daily life.

Formula

Result = (Percentage / 100) × Number

Example Calculation

  • What is 25% of ₹4,800? → (25/100) × 4,800 = ₹1,200
  • What is 18% GST on ₹5,000? → (18/100) × 5,000 = ₹900
  • What is 75% of 120 marks? → (75/100) × 120 = 90 marks

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the Percentage (e.g., 25).
  2. Enter the Number you want to find the percentage of.
  3. Click Calculate to see the instant result.

FAQs

How do I find what percentage one number is of another?

Use: (Part / Whole) × 100. Example: 45 out of 200 = (45/200) × 100 = 22.5%.

What is 20% of 1000?

(20/100) × 1000 = 200.
