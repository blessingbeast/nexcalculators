Business travel, daily commutes, and road trips all come with a hidden cost: the expense per mile driven. This Cost Per Mile Calculator helps you track your vehicle efficiency and accurately budget for your next journey.

What is Cost Per Mile?

Cost per mile (CPM) is an efficiency metric that measures your vehicle's fuel expenditure for every mile of travel. While logistics companies use this to track fleet profitability, individual drivers use it to understand their fuel economy and decide if a particular trip is worth the expense. It is a more practical number than "Miles Per Gallon" alone.

Formula

Cost Per Mile = Total Cost / Total Distance

Total Cost = Distance / Fuel Efficiency × Price per Unit of Fuel

Example

Scenario: You drive 300 miles and spend ₹4,500 on fuel.

  • Total Distance = 300 Miles
  • Total Cost = ₹4,500
  • Cost Per Mile = ₹4,500 / 300 = ₹15 per Mile

How to Use

  1. Enter Total Distance: The length of your trip in miles.
  2. Enter Total Fuel Cost: What you spent on fuel for that trip.
  3. Click Calculate: Instantly see your average cost for every mile traveled.
