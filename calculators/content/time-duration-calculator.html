What Is a Time Duration Calculator?

A time duration calculator finds the total elapsed time between a start time and an end time. Unlike simple subtraction, it correctly handles cases where the end time is before the start time (overnight shifts), making it perfect for night shifts, flight durations, and event timing.

Time Duration Formula

Duration = End Time − Start Time

If End Time < Start Time (overnight): Duration = (24:00 − Start Time) + End Time

Example: 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM = (24:00 − 22:00) + 6:00 = 2 + 6 = 8 hours

Example Calculation

Scenario 1 — Regular Day Shift: Clock in at 9:00 AM, clock out at 5:30 PM.

  • Duration = 5:30 PM − 9:00 AM = 8 hours 30 minutes

Scenario 2 — Night Shift: Start at 10:00 PM, end at 6:00 AM.

  • Duration = (24:00 − 22:00) + 6:00 = 2 + 6 = 8 hours

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can I calculate overnight shift duration?

Yes. If your start time is 10 PM and end time is 6 AM, the calculator automatically adds 24 hours to compute correctly.

2. Does this include seconds?

This calculator works with hours and minutes. For second-level precision, use a stopwatch app.

3. How many hours is 9 AM to 5 PM?

Exactly 8 hours. 9 AM to 5 PM = 17:00 − 09:00 = 8 hours.

4. How do I convert minutes to hours?

Divide minutes by 60. E.g., 90 minutes = 1.5 hours = 1 hour 30 minutes.

5. Is this time duration calculator free?

Yes, completely free. No login required, calculations happen in your browser.
