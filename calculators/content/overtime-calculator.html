What is Overtime Pay?

Overtime pay is the additional compensation paid to employees for hours worked beyond their standard schedule (usually 40 hours per week). In many jurisdictions, this is legally mandated at 1.5 times (Time-and-a-Half) or 2 times (Double Time) the regular hourly rate. This tool helps you verify your paycheck and understand the value of your extra effort.

Formula

Overtime Pay = Hourly Rate × Overtime Hours × Multiplier

Total Pay = Regular Salary + Overtime Pay

Example

Scenario: You earn ₹400/hour and worked 8 hours of overtime at a 1.5x rate.

  • Hourly Rate = ₹400
  • Overtime Rate = ₹400 × 1.5 = ₹600
  • Overtime Pay = 8 × ₹600 = ₹4,800

How to Use

  1. Hourly Rate: Your base pay for one hour of work.
  2. Overtime Hours: The number of extra hours worked in the period.
  3. Multiplier: The multiplier for overtime (usually 1.5).
  4. Click Calculate: Instantly see your total overtime earnings.
