Pricing your products correctly is the difference between a thriving business and a failing one. This Markup Calculator helps you determine the ideal selling price based on your cost and desired profit margin, ensuring you remain competitive and profitable.

What is Markup?

Markup is the amount added to the cost price of goods to cover overhead and profit. It is usually expressed as a percentage of the cost price. While similar to margin, markup focuses on the cost-up side of pricing, whereas margin focuses on the revenue-down side of profit.

Formula

Markup Amount = Cost × (Markup % / 100)

Selling Price = Cost + Markup Amount

Example

Scenario: You buy a shirt for ₹800 and want a 40% markup.

  • Cost = ₹800
  • Markup % = 40%
  • Markup Amount = ₹800 × 0.40 = ₹320
  • Selling Price = ₹800 + ₹320 = ₹1,120

How to Use

  1. Enter Cost Price: What you paid for the item or service.
  2. Enter Markup %: The percentage of profit you want to add.
  3. Click Calculate: Our tool shows the selling price and the total profit amount.
