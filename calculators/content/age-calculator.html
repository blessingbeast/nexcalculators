Formula: How Age is Calculated

While manual subtraction looks easy, it is prone to errors during "borrowing" for months and days. Our tool uses the following chronological logic:

Years = (Current Year - Birth Year)

If (Current Month < Birth Month) OR (Current Month == Birth Month AND Current Day < Birth Day), then subtract 1 from Years.

Months and Days are then calculated based on the previous month's total days.
