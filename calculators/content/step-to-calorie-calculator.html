Your daily step count is a powerful health metric. Our step to calorie calculator converts your pedometer or fitness tracker's step count into approximate calories burned — factoring in your body weight for accurate, personalised results.

What is a Step to Calorie Calculator?

A step to calorie calculator converts the number of steps you've walked into an estimated calorie burn. Unlike heart-rate-based calculations, it uses a straightforward formula based on the average energy cost of walking, adjusted for body weight. It's ideal for fitness tracker users who want to understand the caloric value of their daily step goals.

Formula

Calories per Step ≈ 0.04 × Weight (kg) / 70 (normalised)

Total Calories = Steps × Calories per Step

Simplified: Calories ≈ Steps × 0.04 (for ~70 kg adult)

Example Calculation

  • 10,000 steps. Weight: 70 kg.
  • Calories = 10,000 × 0.04 = 400 calories (approx.)
  • For 80 kg: Calories = 10,000 × (0.04 × 80/70) = 457 calories

FAQs

How many steps equal 1 km?

On average, 1 kilometer = ~1,300 steps (shorter stride) to 1,500 steps (longer stride). Taller individuals cover more distance per step.

Is 10,000 steps really the target?

10,000 steps is a popular goal originating from a 1960s Japanese marketing campaign. Research actually suggests 7,500-8,000 steps/day provides substantial health benefits, with diminishing returns beyond that for non-athletes.
