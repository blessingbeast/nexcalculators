Retirement Calculator: Secure Your Second Innings

Retirement is the only financial goal you cannot get a loan for. You can borrow for a house, a car, or education, but no bank will lend you money for your old age. You rely entirely on the corpus you build today. This Retirement Calculator estimates the massive number you need (Your "Number") to maintain your lifestyle when the paycheck stops.

The 25x Rule (FIRE Movement)

A general rule of thumb: You need 25 times your annual expenses to retire.
If you spend ₹10 Lakhs a year, you need ₹2.5 Crores.
This assumes you withdraw 4% annually (Safe Withdrawal Rate) and the corpus lasts 30 years.

The Inflation Factor

Currently spending ₹50,000/month? In 20 years (at 6% inflation), you will need ₹1,60,000/month to buy the same things. Your corpus target must account for this future value.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. When to start?

First paycheck. The power of compounding works best over long periods.

2. How much to save?

15-20% of income is recommended.

3. EPF enough?

Usually no. EPF covers basics, but rarely lifestyle. You need Equity (MF/Stocks).

4. Health cover?

Crucial. Company insurance stops at retirement. Buy personal insurance early.

5. Bucket Strategy?

Keep 3 years expenses in Debt (Liquid). Rest in Equity for growth.

6. NPS?

Great for retirement due to lock-in and tax efficiency.

7. Debt-free?

Aim to pay off Home Loan before retiring.

8. Life expectancy?

Plan for living till 85 or 90. Living too long is a financial risk!

9. SWP?

Systematic Withdrawal Plan from Mutual Funds generates monthly pension.

10. Reverse Mortgage?

Last resort. Mortgaging house to bank for monthly income.
