What is a GST Exclusive Calculator?

A GST exclusive calculator adds the applicable Goods & Services Tax to a base (pre-tax) price. When a price is listed "exclusive of GST," the buyer pays more than the advertised price. As a seller, business, or B2B buyer, you need to add GST accurately to every invoice, quote, and purchase order.

Formula

GST Amount = Base Price × GST Rate / 100

Total Invoice Price = Base Price + GST Amount

CGST = SGST = GST Amount / 2 (for intra-state transactions)

Example Calculation

A software consulting firm charges ₹50,000 for a project (exclusive of 18% GST).

  • GST Amount = ₹50,000 × 18/100 = ₹9,000
  • Total Invoice Amount = ₹50,000 + ₹9,000 = ₹59,000
  • CGST (9%) = ₹4,500 | SGST (9%) = ₹4,500

FAQs

When is GST charged exclusively vs. inclusively?

B2B invoices almost always show GST exclusively (base + tax = total). Retail consumer goods typically show a GST-inclusive MRP printed on the packaging.

What GST rate applies to services in India?

Most professional services (consulting, legal, IT, accounting) fall under the 18% GST slab. Healthcare, education, and some financial services are exempt.

Do I need a GST number to charge GST?

Yes. Only GST-registered businesses (turnover above ₹20L for services, ₹40L for goods) can charge and collect GST. Registration is also mandatory for e-commerce sellers regardless of turnover.
