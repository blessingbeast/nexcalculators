What is a Work Hours Calculator?

A work hours calculator is a simple yet powerful tool used to determine the total duration of a work shift. It subtracts the start time from the end time and further removes any non-working hours (like lunch breaks) to arrive at the "net" hours worked. This is critical for accurate payroll, freelance billing, and shift analysis.

Formula

Total Hours = (End Time - Start Time) - Break Duration

Example: (5:00 PM - 9:00 AM) - 1 Hour = 7 Hours total.

Example

Scenario: You started work at 8:30 AM and finished at 5:15 PM, taking a 45-minute lunch break.

  • Elapsed Time = 8 Hours and 45 Minutes
  • Break Time = 45 Minutes
  • Net Work Hours = 8 Hours

How to Use

  1. Start Time: The exact time you clocked in.
  2. End Time: The exact time you clocked out.
  3. Break Duration: Total minutes of unpaid breaks taken.
  4. Click Calculate: Get your total working time in hours and minutes.
